A Letter For You

🌹

April 7, 2026

My dearest Sora,

One year. Three hundred and sixty-five sunrises. And somehow, every single one of them has been more beautiful because you were there to share it with me.

The day we met changed everything. I don't think I fully understood what it meant to have someone light up your world — not until you walked into mine. Your laugh, your warmth, the way your eyes hold a whole universe in them — you turned ordinary days into something I never want to forget.

You are my favourite person. Not just on the easy, golden days, but on every in-between moment too — the quiet evenings, the silly arguments we always laugh about later, the adventures big and small. With you, even the simplest things feel magical.

I cherish every single memory we have built together, and I promise I will keep building more with you — more laughter, more adventures, more late-night talks about everything and nothing at all.

Here we are at one year, and I can already see forever when I look at you. Thank you for choosing me every single day. Thank you for being you. Thank you for being mine.

Here's to us, Sora. Here's to every year still to come. 💕

Forever yours,
Shin 🌹
